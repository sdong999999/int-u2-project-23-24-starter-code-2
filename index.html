Legislation



Legislation at the federal, state, and local levels has attempted to address gun violence through measures including restricting gun purchases by youth and other “high-risk” populations, establishing waiting periods for gun purchases, establishing gun buyback programs, and law enforcement and policing strategies to combat gun violence. Tough sentences for gun law violators, education programs for parents and children, and community outreach programs.
